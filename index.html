Harmony, Humanity, Opportunity (H₂O) ...

"we leave the ocean better than we found it "

I have founded this company with Our company, (H₂O), offers a new life to marine waste by turning them into affordable products that further promote sustainable and healthy living. We aim to address the deficiencies of current methods, which are characterized by insufficient public involvement and nonstandard marine debris disposal, with an intention to provide a simplified and systemized channel for people to participate in the ocean cleanup effort. By teaming up with existing garbage removal institutions, prominently with the Ocean Cleanup company, we combine our resources and technology to recycle portions of the pacific garbage.
